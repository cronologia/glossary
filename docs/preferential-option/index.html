Preferential option for the poor

The formula at the heart of Latin American liberation theology, consolidated by the CELAM conferences of Medellín (1968) and Puebla (1979) and since absorbed into papal teaching. Its interpretation — pastoral priority vs political program — is precisely what the 1980s disputes were about.[1]
