Encontro / Encuentro

'meeting' (Portuguese/Spanish); capitalized, the numbered meeting series of the Foro de São Paulo and of the Grupo de Puebla

Ordinary words for 'meeting' that double as proper names of two different numbered series. The gatherings of the Foro de São Paulo are counted as Encontros/Encuentros — 27 between São Paulo 1990 and 2024, e.g. the XVIII Encuentro (Caracas, 2012). The Grupo de Puebla counts its own Encuentros from the founding Encuentro Latinoamericano ProgresivaMente (Puebla, Mexico, July 2019) — ten by mid-2024. A bare 'X Encontro' or 'Encuentro' citation is therefore ambiguous between two organizations founded 29 years apart; the chronologies always name the series.[1] [2] [3]
