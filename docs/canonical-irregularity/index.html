Canonical irregularity ('no canonical status')

The Holy See's characterization of the SSPX's position between 1975 and 2026: the Society possessed 'no canonical status in the Church' and its ministers 'do not legitimately exercise any ministry' — Benedict XVI's 2009 wording — while officials in the same era distinguished this irregular condition from formal schism. A status label, not a doctrinal verdict; always date it.[1]
