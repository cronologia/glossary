Tariqa (and the Maryamiyya)

A Sufi order — a Muslim initiatic brotherhood under a shaykh. The Maryamiyya is the tariqa founded by the perennialist Frithjof Schuon; the documented membership of Rama Coomaraswamy — sedevacantist author and sometime professor at the SSPX's US seminary — is the reason a Sufi term appears in a Catholic-traditionalism glossary.[1]
