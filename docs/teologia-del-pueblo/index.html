Teología del pueblo

The Argentine 'theology of the people': a current born from the same post-conciliar ferment as liberation theology but centered on popular culture and religiosity rather than class analysis. It formed Jorge Bergoglio — which is why readings of Pope Francis as a 'liberation theologian' are contested; the Cronologia sites keep the labels apart.[1]
