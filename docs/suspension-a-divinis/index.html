Suspension a divinis

A censure that forbids a cleric the exercise of the power of orders — celebrating Mass, administering sacraments (can. 1333). Marcel Lefebvre was suspended a divinis in July 1976 after ordaining priests against an explicit prohibition; Ernesto Cardenal was suspended in 1984 for holding government office, a suspension lifted by Francis in 2019.[1] [2] [3]
