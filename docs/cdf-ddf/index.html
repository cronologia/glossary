CDF / DDF

Congregation (now Dicastery) for the Doctrine of the Faith; historically the Holy Office

The Vatican's doctrinal watchdog: the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith, renamed Dicastery (DDF) in the 2022 curial reform. Author of the documents both chronologies pivot on — Libertatis Nuntius (1984) and Libertatis Conscientia (1986), the Boff and Sobrino notifications, and the 2026 SSPX decree.[1]
