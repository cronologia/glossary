Pia unio (pious union)

An associational form of canon law under which the SSPX was erected in 1970 by Bishop Charrière of Lausanne, Geneva and Fribourg, on an ad experimentum basis — the diocesan approval whose 1975 withdrawal (disputed by the Society) opened the canonical conflict.[1]
