CEBs (comunidades eclesiais de base)

Grassroots 'base ecclesial communities' — small neighborhood groups reading the Gospel in connection with daily life — strongest in Brazil, where they carried liberation theology's pastoral practice and, per attributed accounts, fed cadres into the Workers' Party in the 1980s. Estimates of their peak numbers vary widely between sources.[1]
